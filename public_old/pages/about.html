About Me



I am an 18-year-old from Philadelphia and I am currently a prefrosh planning to study Computer Science at Cornell University.


I was a 9-year-old who loved taking apart toys when I discovered videos of batch programming on YouTube. Slowly, on my dad's old Athlon II computer, I learned about the infinite possibilities of software.


I was never the best at school or academics, I'm just a super curious guy. Ever since my initial exposure to programming, I have continued to explore many facets of technology, including: web development, competitive programming, computer hardware, and machine learning, and the blockchain.


In addition to being passionate about technology, I'm eagar to challenge myself to do things I've never accomplished before and I strive to improve on my skills and knowledge every day.
