raneto

Raneto is an open-source nodejs knowledge base powered by markdown. It's very lightweight, and I currently have it hosted at wiki.jakegines.in.

UPDATE 12/18/2022: wiki is down. heroku changed their free shit. looking for a different hosting method.

Heroku

Heroku is the best free method of hosting Raneto that I have found so far. Raneto provides a short guide on heroku hosting here. Unfortunately, Heroku uses an Ephermeral Disk, meaning any changes written to the disk will not persist after the application is restarted. I am currently looking for a way around this.
